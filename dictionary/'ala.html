’ala

High Lulani

‮al

/ˈʔal.ə/

inanimate stem noun

heat

Derived Terms

’alamusa sun’s heat

’alalupumma heat of the fire

Usage Notes

these are just about the only terms of this type. Anything else uses the genitive.

Examples

dusunidisiqa ’ala the heat of his hand
