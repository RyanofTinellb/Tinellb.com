buma

High Lulani

‮Om

/ˈbum.ə/

transitive stem verb

to draw; to write; to project on screen

Thematic Relations

agent subject

theme, goal dative, ni

recipient dative

speech object

Examples

ʔusu kulisa ba’u buma ’ita’i.

1int dat-paper 2dat draw car.
