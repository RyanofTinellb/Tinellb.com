calu

High Lulani

cW

/ˈcal.ʉ/

abstract stem noun

dimension

Definition

One of ten directions in which something can be measured, or one of four ways in which something can be measured.

Hypernyms

’u’ili reality

bitta time

sufi space

maʔu gingla

stem mathematics

exponential; power

Example

kifi calu ra ’anu sahaki.

three power two equal one-reven.

“The third power of two is eight.”
