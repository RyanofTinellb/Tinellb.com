cikki

High Lulani

‮C;K

/ˈcʰik.kɨ/

ambitransitive stem verb

to hear telepathically; to see empathically

Thematic Relations

experiencer subject

stimulus dative

theme object

direction pa

Examples

ʔusu ba’u hulu.

1int 2dat hear.

“I heard you.”

ʔusu hulu fu.

1int hear 2tra.

“I listened to you.”

ʔusu pafilli hulu.

1int towards-2int hear.

“I listened in your direction.”
