ji’i

High Lulani

‮Ji

/ˈɟiʔ.ɨ/

ambitransitive stem verb

to taste; to smell

Thematic Relations

experiencer subject

stimulus dative

theme object

direction pa

Examples

ʔusu ba’u ji’i.

1int 2dat smell.

“I smelt you.”

ʔusu ji’i fu.

1int taste 2tra.

“I tasted you.”

ʔusu pafilli ji’i.

1int towards-2int taste.

“I smelt towards you.”
