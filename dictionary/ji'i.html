ji’i

High Lulani

‮Ji

/ˈɟiʔ.ɨ/

ambitransitive stem verb

to taste; to smell

Thematic Relations

experiencer subject

stimulus dative

theme object

direction pa

Examples

ʔusu ba’u ’usa.

1int 2dat see.

“I smelt you.”

ʔusu ’usa fu.

1int see 2tra.
