ju’i

High Lulani

‮Yi

/ˈɟuʔ.ɨ/

ambitransitive stem verb

link; join; wear

Thematic Relations

patient, theme

subject, object, dative, haru

Example

Suma ju’i sunu.

1tra link cloak.

“I put my cloak on.”

Derived Terms

ju’iduku marry

mathematics

plus

Example

Kifi ju’i nuru ’anu salumi.

Three add four equal seven.

“Three plus four equals seven.”
