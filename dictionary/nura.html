nura

High Lulani

Ir

/ˈnuɹ.ə/

intransitive stem verb

to separate; to move away from someone else; to leave

Thematic Relations

theme subject

source ablative

stimulus dative

[6]Usage Notes

This is a dative verb for volitional separation, ablative for non-volitional separation.

Examples

ʔusu kululani nura.

1int dat-queen leave.

“I left the queen.”

ʔusu kalululani nura.

1int abl-queen leave.

“I was separated from the queen.”
