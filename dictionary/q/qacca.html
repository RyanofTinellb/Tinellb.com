qacca

High Lulani

‮q;c

/ˈŋac.cə/

ambitransitive stem verb

choose; decide

Thematic Relations

agent subject

source ablative, bi

goal dative

theme sarru

patient object

Examples

ʔusu kuli nara cani qacca.

1int dat-com sleep prs;dyn choose.

“I decided to go to sleep.”

ʔusu kaludixxa sarrucijja qacca ’iti.

1int abl-drink instead_of-alcohol choose milk.

“Of the drinks, I chose milk rather than alcohol.”
