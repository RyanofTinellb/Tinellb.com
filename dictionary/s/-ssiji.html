-ssiji

High Lulani

;SJ

/s.sɨ.ɟɨ/

derivational suffix (mathematics → noun)

derives the names of shapes from the number of edges or vertices

Example

kifissiji KF;SJ triangle

nurussiji Iw;SJ quadrilateral
