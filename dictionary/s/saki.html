saki

High Lulani

‮sK

/ˈsakʰ.ɨ/

transitive stem verb

to share

Thematic Relations

agent subject

patient ablative, bi

recipient object, haru, raqu

Example

Suma bipa saki filli.

1tra from-3ina;int share 2int.

“I shared it with you.”

Derived Terms

sakimica to give permission

stem mathematics

1. divided by

Example

Saqikka saki kifi ’anu guhi.

one-zero divide three equal five.

“Fifteen divided by three is five.”

See Also

’anu equal

ju’i plus

tali minus

batu multiply


2. denotes a fraction that is the reciprocal of an integer

Examples

saki ra half

saki kifi third
