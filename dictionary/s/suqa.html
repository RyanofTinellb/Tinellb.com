suqa

High Lulani

zq

/ˈsuŋ.ə/

intransitive stem verb

to be ready; to be completed; to be prepared

Thematic Roles

patient subject

agent ablative

Example

ʔusu suqapakka si’apa.

.uz / zqp;k / Sap.

1int prepare-abp meal.

“I prepared the meal.”

Notes

the patient must be inanimate.
