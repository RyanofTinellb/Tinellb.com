su’a

High Lulani

‮za

/ˌsuʔ.ə/

stem pronoun

self; reflexive particle

Usage Notes

replaces third-person pronouns, if necessary, to refer to the subject of the sentence. Do not take case markers. Can be used as genitive, but only as a suffix; in this case, covers both alienable and inalienable possession.
