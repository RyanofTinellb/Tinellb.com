tina

High Lulani

‮Tn

/ˈtʰin.ə/

intransitive stem verb

to differ from

Thematic Relations

theme subject

stimulus ablative, haru, xidu

Examples

ʔusu sacu tina.

1int 2abl differ.

“I’m not you.”

ʔusu xidufilli tina.

1int far-2int differ.

“I’m nothing like you.”
