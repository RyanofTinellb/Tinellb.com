Comparatives


[4]Comparatives
The comparative marker is an adposition su than placed on the noun being compared against. For "little more", the qualifier tuci half-do is affixed to the verb, for "a lot more", the qualifier is rali intensely.
[4]Equalatives
The equalative marker is ka as, an adposition.
[4]Superlative
The superlative marker, contrary to the other two, is a particle placed before the verb.
