dat - dative

drv - derivative

dtv - dative promotion

dua - dual number

dyn - dynamic

fut - future

gen - genitive

ger - gerund

gno - gnomic

imp - imperfect

imv - imperative

ina - inanimate

inc - inceptive

neg - negative

nom - nominative

npt - non-past

nmn - nominaliser

ord - ordinal

pos - possessive

prf - perfect

prs - present

pst - past

psv - passive (object promotion)

qal - qualifier

que - question

rel - relativiser

sjv - subjunctive

sta - stative

tra - transitive case
