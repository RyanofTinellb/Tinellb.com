Particular Clauses

Experiences

The modal da’aru experience can be used to show experiences.

Fu ’usa kuddu da’aru qixa?

2tra see rain experience pst;dyn?

“Have you ever seen the rain?”

Fu lu’isamma ’usu da’aru labu?

2tra love-true_love 1int experience fut;dyn

“Could you ever love me?”
