About the Asteroid Belt

This is a simulation of something like the “Asteroid Belt” in Star Wars, written in a javascript library called p5.

It consists of a random number of bodies with a random gravity. The barycentre of the system is always centred on the screen. Two bodies that eclipse each other do not affect each other - this is to prevent infinite speeds as the distance between them approaches zero, in accordance with the inverse square law.

Click on the image to check it out: It looks best in full screen, and hit refresh in your browser to get a new system.
